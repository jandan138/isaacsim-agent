## 5. Summary of Visual Polish Rules for NeurIPS/CVPR Aesthetics

*   **No Drop Shadows:** Use a 1px solid border (#E5E7EB) around elements instead of drop shadows to create depth. This guarantees clean printing.
*   **Gradients:** If used, restrict to a vertical 90-degree linear gradient within the exact same hue. For example, the Blue Planner box goes from `#3B82F6` at the top to `#2563EB` at the bottom. **No cross-color gradients.**
*   **Alignment:** Nodes 1, 2, 5, and 6 must be perfectly top-aligned. Nodes 3 and 4 are taller but share the same vertical center line as the rest.
*   **Padding:** Ensure at least 30px of negative space between the loopback arrow and the title text of the bottom-tier insets. White space is critical for academic diagram legibility.
